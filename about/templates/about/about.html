About Us

Earth has close to 8 billion people and less than 600 people have been lucky enough to wake up outside of Earth’s atmosphere.

In 2020, Space Adventure Agency opened the door for private citizens to explore space – a destination that holds the future for humanity – and we can do the same for you!

About the Trips

A trip to space is not a an everyday event. So before a trip we’ll work with you to identify your needs and priorities, in order to customize your training and preparation program accordingly.

By developing a spaceflight program to match your interests and focus on activities that are meaningful to you, we’ll enhance your in-space experience.
